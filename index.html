Elenka

Elenka is an up and coming german electro-pop singer. This dress was supposed to reflect a butterfly coming out its cocoon, made from many different fabrics - draped on to the singer with few changes because of the crazy Icelandic weather.
https://soundcloud.com/elenkamusic

Zebra Katz

Zebra Katz is an american underground hip hop sensation and a visionary. The multi coloured fur coat was made from old cut-outs from a special coloured icelandic sheep skin.
http://www.zebrakatz.com

Cale Glendening

Alexander Kirchner

Alexander Kirchner has been working as a freelance designer, stylist and producer - including sewing and knitting his own designs for a number of years. Kirchner has collaborated with other designers, the music industry, and artists of all genres, in designing, producing and styling one of a kind, elite projects for individual customers and high-end production companies. From creating unique wedding dresses to authentic costumes for music videos, Kirchner has had his hand in almost every aspect of fashion - Now he extends his scope as a head designer and a visionary of his own brand;
